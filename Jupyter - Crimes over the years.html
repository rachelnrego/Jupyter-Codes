Out of the property crime vehicle theft seems to have the highest numbers over the years. In case of vehicle theft the highest between 1987 and 1992. The lowest of the Property crimes is burglary, the increase being between 1987 and 1992. In case of violent crime murder has the lowest rate and the highest is aggravated assault. But the spread of robbery is higher than any other violent crimes.All the crimes have a peak increase between the years 1987 and 1992. Overall there is was an increasing trend till 1992 post which there has been a reduction in all the crimes.
